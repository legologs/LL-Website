About Us

LegoLogs was created by two middle schoolers messing around with the programming language "HTML" hypertext markup language.



Cole (Founder)


I am a TFOL (teen fan of LEGO) and LEGO is my hobby that I am most proud of. I love building MOCs (my own creations) and also love building plain LEGO sets. But, every once and awhile I don't know what to build. This is why I came up with the idea to make a website where people can view other creations and post their own. Alex (Co-Founder) taught me HTML which is a programming language used to create websites.
